💡 How to Develop OLQs

Leadership

Take initiative in group activities, participate in NCC, lead college events, practice group discussions.

Communication

Join debate/elocution clubs, practice extempore speaking, read aloud daily, learn to express ideas clearly.

Self Confidence

Set and achieve small goals, take on challenges, avoid overthinking, maintain good physical fitness.

Team Spirit

Participate in team sports, volunteer for group projects, learn to value others' contributions.

Initiative

Don't wait to be told — act first. Start projects, help others without being asked, solve problems proactively.

Responsibility

Accept accountability for mistakes, follow through on commitments, be reliable in all roles.
