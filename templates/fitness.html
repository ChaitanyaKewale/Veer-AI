📅 12-Week Training Plan

Weeks 1-4
Foundation Phase
  • Run: 2-3 km daily (easy pace)
  • Pushups: 3 × 15
  • Situps: 3 × 20
  • Pullups: 3 × 3-5
  • Rest: Sunday
Weeks 5-8
Build Phase
  • Run: 4-5 km daily + intervals
  • Pushups: 4 × 25
  • Situps: 4 × 30
  • Pullups: 4 × 6-8
  • Weekend: 8 km long run
Weeks 9-12
Peak Phase
  • Run: 1.6 km timed practice daily
  • Pushups: 5 × 30
  • Situps: 5 × 35
  • Pullups: 5 × 10+
  • Full fitness test weekly

🥗 Nutrition for Aspirants

🌅
Pre-Workout

Banana, dates, or light oats 30 min before exercise. Stay hydrated.

🍳
Breakfast

Eggs (3-4), whole grain bread/roti, milk, fruits. High protein start.

🍱
Lunch

Rice/roti + dal/chicken + vegetables + salad. Balanced macros.

🥛
Post-Workout

Protein shake or paneer/eggs within 30 min for muscle recovery.

🌙
Dinner

Light meal: soup + roti + vegetables. Avoid heavy carbs at night.

💧
Hydration

3-4 litres water daily. Coconut water/nimbu pani after heavy exercise.
