🪖 Indian Army — Entry Routes

Serving since 1895 | 13 lakh+ active personnel

NDA
Eligibility: 10+2 pass, Age 16.5-19.5 yrs (Male/Female)
Process: UPSC Exam → SSB → Medical
Commission: Permanent Commission as Lieutenant
Training: 3 yrs NDA + 1.5 yrs IMA
CDS IMA
Eligibility: Graduate any stream, Age 19-24 yrs
Process: UPSC CDS → SSB → Medical
Commission: Permanent Commission as Lieutenant
Training: 18 months at IMA, Dehradun
TES (Technical Entry Scheme)
Eligibility: 10+2 PCM with 70%+, Age 16.5-19.5 yrs
Process: SSB (no written exam)
Commission: Permanent Commission
Training: 5 years (B.Tech + Military training)
NCC Special Entry
Eligibility: Graduate with NCC 'C' Certificate (Army Wing 'B' Grade)
Process: SSB only (no written exam)
Commission: Short Service Commission (14 yrs)
Training: 49 weeks at IMA/OTA
CDS OTA (SSC)
Eligibility: Graduate any stream, Age 19-25 yrs
Process: UPSC CDS → SSB → Medical
Commission: Short Service Commission (14 yrs), option for PC
Training: 11 months at OTA Chennai
Agniveer (Army)
Eligibility: 10th/12th pass (varies by trade), Age 17.5-21 yrs
Process: Written + Physical + Medical
Commission: 4-year contract, 25% retained
Pay: Rs 30,000–40,000/month + Seva Nidhi

🚢 Indian Navy — Entry Routes

Protecting 7,516 km coastline | 67,000+ personnel

NDA (Naval Wing)
Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics & Maths, Age 16.5-19.5 yrs
Process: UPSC NDA → SSB → Medical
Commission: Permanent Commission as Sub Lieutenant
Training: 3 yrs NDA + 3 yrs INA Ezhimala
CDS (INA Entry)
Eligibility: BE/B.Tech Engineering Graduate
Process: UPSC CDS → SSB → Medical
Commission: Permanent Commission
Training: 3 years at INA Ezhimala, Kerala
Agniveer (Navy)
Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM (for technical), Age 17.5-21 yrs
Process: Written + Physical Fitness Test + Medical
Trades: Senior Secondary Recruit (SSR), Matric Recruit (MR)
Pay: Rs 30,000–40,000/month

✈️ Indian Air Force — Entry Routes

4th largest air force | 1.4 lakh+ personnel | 900+ aircraft

NDA (Air Force Wing)
Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics & Maths, Age 16.5-19.5 yrs
Process: UPSC NDA → AFSB → Medical (Pilot standard)
Commission: Permanent Commission as Flying Officer
Training: 3 yrs NDA + 74 weeks AFA Dundigal
AFCAT (Flying Branch)
Eligibility: Graduate with PCM at 10+2, Age 20-24 yrs
Process: AFCAT written + AFSB + Medical
Commission: Permanent Commission (Flying)
Training: 74 weeks at Air Force Academy
AFCAT (Technical/Ground Duty)
Eligibility: Engineering graduate / relevant degree, Age 20-26 yrs
Process: AFCAT + EKT (for technical) + AFSB
Branches: Aeronautical Engg (M), Aeronautical Engg (E), Admin, Accounts, Education, Logistics, Met
Commission: Permanent/Short Service Commission
Agniveer Vayu
Eligibility: 10+2 (Science/Arts/Commerce based on trade), Age 17.5-21 yrs
Process: Written exam + Physical + Medical
Trades: Science (Technical), Science (Non-Tech), Others
Pay: Rs 30,000–40,000/month
